Hello! :)


I am Eula Paula, a professional freelance makeup artist living in Manila, Philippines. I have always been fascinated with colors. It is not surprising that I fell in love with makeup while attending the School of Fashion and the Arts’ Personal Makeup Class in 2012. Shortly after, I trained in Maquillage Professional School of Makeup Artisty, Modules 1 and 2 focused on Beauty, Photography and High Fashion Makeup. Then, learned Airbrush in HD Academy. In 2014, I attended the International Makeup Artist Trade Show (IMATS) in Sydney, Australia where I learned a lot from international makeup artists like Rae Morris, Bennett and many others. I also trained with Donna Mee, Los Angeles based makeup artist while in Sydney. In 2017, I attended a class in Temptu Head Quarters in New York, USA with the temptu head makeup artist, Sylvia Pichler for Airbrush Techniques. My style is more on fresh, natural makeup that glows. Nothing is more fulfilling to me as an artist than making people feel beautiful and giving my utmost care and service to my clients. Me in 4 words: friendly, sensitive, understanding and professional. ♥
